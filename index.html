The Ultimate Productivity Companion

[cite: 2]

Side Panel Notes for Google Keep™ integrates your notes directly into your browsing workflow, providing a seamless experience that feels native to Chrome.[cite: 2]

  • Native Side Panel: Open Google Keep in Chrome's side panel for a perfect split-screen view while you work, study, or browse.[cite: 2]
  • Standalone Window: "Pop out" your notes into a dedicated, sleek window that stays exactly where you left it.[cite: 2]
  • Smart Window Memory: The extension remembers your preferred window size and position automatically.[cite: 2]
  • Right-Click Access: Quickly open your sidebar from anywhere on the web using the context menu.[cite: 2]
  • Compact View: Optimized layout ensures your notes are always easy to read and manage, even in narrow panels.[cite: 2]

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2, 2026

[cite: 1]
Side Panel Notes for Google Keep™ is committed to absolute privacy. This extension does NOT collect, store, or take any kind of data from you.[cite: 2]

1. No Data Collection

I do not take any kind of data.[cite: 2] This extension does not track your browsing history, does not collect personal information, and does not access your Google Keep data (all access is managed securely by Google).[cite: 2]

2. Secure Storage

Settings—such as window size and position—are stored locally on your device.[cite: 1, 2] Your notes are yours alone, kept securely on Google’s servers, and remain inaccessible to the developer.[cite: 2]

3. Use of Permissions

  • Native Side Panel: Used to display Google Keep for a perfect split-screen experience.[cite: 2]
  • Window Management: Used to provide the standalone window feature and remember your preferred window size.[cite: 2]
  • Context Menu: Used to allow you to quickly open the side panel from anywhere on the web.[cite: 2]

I do not share any information with third parties, as no information is collected in the first place.[cite: 1, 2]
