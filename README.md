# Side Panel Notes for Google Keep™

**Side Panel Notes for Google Keep™** is a lightweight, high-performance Chrome extension that integrates Google Keep directly into your browsing experience. Access your notes, checklists, and reminders without ever leaving your current tab.

## 🚀 Features

- **Side Panel Integration**: Open Google Keep in the native Chrome side panel for a seamless, split-screen experience.
- **Standalone Window**: "Pop out" your notes into a dedicated window that stays on top of other applications.
- **Context Menu Support**: Right-click anywhere on any page to quickly open your notes.
- **Window Memory**: Remembers your standalone window's size and position for a personalized setup.
- **Privacy First**: Zero data collection. Your notes stay between you and Google.

## 🛠 Installation

### For Developers (Manual Install)
1. Clone this repository or download the source code.
2. Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Enable **Developer mode** (top-right corner).
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the extension directory.

## 📖 How to Use

1. **Open Sidebar**: Click the extension icon in the toolbar or right-click and select "Open Keep Sidebar".
2. **Switch to Window**: Click the **Pop out** (orange arrow) button at the bottom-left of the sidebar to switch to a standalone window.
3. **Close**: Use the **Close** (X) button at the bottom-left to quickly dismiss the sidebar.

## 🔒 Privacy

I respect your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. All interactions occur directly between your browser and Google Keep.
